About us

International Day of Yoga, celebrated annually on 21 June since its inception in 2015, is an international day for yoga was declared unanimously by the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA). Amrita Yoga helps coordinate the promotion, propagation and execution of all International Day of Yoga (IDY) activities in India by the Ministry of AYUSH and the MDNIY, an institution under the Ministry. In 2015, in India, Amritapuri ashram, over 54 Amrita Vidyalayam schools throughout India participated in the first annual event, along with more than 150 Ashram Centers and Amrita institutions worldwide. More than 300 places held independent celebrations with Amrita Yoga ambassadors in 2016. IDY2017 was organized and celebrated in around 350 locations across the globe. Amrita Yoga Director was invited by the Indian Embassy, Oslo, Norway to inaugurate and celebrate IDY2017.
